Why Choose My Therapies?

About Me

Hi, my name is Holly Wells. I am a fully certified, accredited and insured, therapist, registered with the Complimentary Medicine Association (CMA). My aim is to provide a caring, professional and individual service to my clients.

Holistic Techniques

Instead of just treating the symptoms of a disease, my complimentary therapies look to address the root of the problem, by aligning your physical, spiritual and emotional self. My treatments are not a replacement for conventional medicine, but work in symmetry with it to promote healing, health and a sense of wellbeing.

Healing Space

Come and be nurtured, and experience respite, from your hectic, modern life, in my serene log cabin. Located in my beautiful garden, in the quiet country town of Knighton. It is the perfect place to relax and enjoy some "me time".

Benefits

My holistic complimentary therapies can help to improve many aspects of health. These include; stress, anxiety, depression, aches and pains, tension and fatigue. These benefits are achieved in a natural way, and can reduce your reliance on over-the-counter medicines.
